Polo by the Pier

Date: Saturday 8th of February

Location: Portsea Pier

Website: PoloByThePier.com

How to sign up: Ask your team mamager if your team will be participating, if not contact us and we can put you in contact with another team that's participating.

Some Extra Info:
Polo By The Pier has been the end of year water polo celebration for all of Victoria, this time it will mark the launch of 2019!
This year the event will have a Men's, Social Men's, Women's and U14 Mixed Tournament
See you by the Pier

Summer Splash

Date: 5th of February to the 25th of March

Website: SummerSplash.MonashUniWaterPolo.com.au

How to sign up: Individual and team entries are both welcome. Contact us and we will enter your team or be in touch to sort you (and your friends!) into a team.

Some Extra Info:
A For all the Water Polo teams and players who just can't wait for competition to resume, Moansh are putting on a Summer Social Competition specially for you! The only requirement for players is to be registered with Water Polo Victoria.
