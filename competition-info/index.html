UniSport Nationals (Inter-Varsity Uni Games)

Date: 07 - 13 September 2024

Website: unisport.com.au/nationals-water-polo

UniSport Nationals provide an opportunity for students at our 42 member universities from across Australia to compete head to head in a number of national standard sporting competitions. In 2024, Water Polo will be contested in both men's and women's competitions at Nationals in Canberra.
