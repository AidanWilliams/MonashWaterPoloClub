Some details about the various water polo competitions Monash participates in

All dates for 2022

Polo by the Pier

Date: Saturday 5th of February

Location: Portsea Pier

Website: PoloByThePier.com

How to sign up: Ask your team manager if your team will be participating, if not contact us and we can put you in contact with another team that's participating.

Some Extra Info:
Polo by the Pier is back for 2022. After a year off due to COVID-19, PBTP is set to resume on February 5th. The event this year will be held in support of colours4kara, a cause that is close to the hearts of many in the waterpolo community. Registration is now open for Mens, Women and Social competition. After the event, there will be a BBQ and drinks at the Portsea Bowls club.

Ballarat Junior Waterpolo Tournament

Dates: One weekend in October

Location: Ballarat Aquatic & Lifestyle Centre

Some Extra Info:
A competition put on by Monash Uni Waterpolo that invites junior teams from all over Victoria to participate in a two day tournament held in Ballarat and consiting of not just waterpolo but fun social events and dinners.
