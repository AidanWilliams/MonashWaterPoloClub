Established in 1984 The Monash Uni Water Polo Club welcomes members of all standards, experience and background. We train three times a week at the Doug Ellis Pool located on Campus at Monash University in Clayton Victoria, Australia. The University is easily accessible via the Monash Freeway (part of Citylink) from Melbourne and all suburbs. We field teams in competitions run by Victorian Water Polo as well as other smaller competitions. Monash has a history of attracting players from all over the world to play for us. Past players have come from the U.K., Canada, the U.S., Hong Kong, Malaysia, Singapore, Sri Lanka, Ireland, South Africa, Sweden, Norway, Holland and Israel. We welcome both new and experienced players whether they be from Melbourne, or anywhere else in the World.

Get Involved

Our club has teams of all levels for interested competitors both male and female. Anyone with basic swimming competence willing to work hard to learn the skillset of the game is very welcome to come along to training! In total there are 7 men’s leagues and 3 women’s leagues.

​Our club has no formal procedure for new members to join. Interested players are invited to contact us and we'll invite you to come along to training, when ready to start competing we'll find a suitable team and the team manager of that team can then assist with the Water Polo Victoria registration process.
