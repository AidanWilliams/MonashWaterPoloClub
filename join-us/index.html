Why Join Us?

Why Join Us?

Easy,

we are a young club club but have thirty years of experience. We love playing water polo and having fun (not that the two are mutually exclusive), we have multiple teams of all skill levels and strive to create strong relationships both in and out of the pool. So whether you are thinking about trying out water polo for the first time or are have competed in the Olympics you'll feel at home at Monash.

How to Join?

If you have Facebook just send us a message using the chat in the right hand corner of the screen. Don't have Facebook? Send us an email by clicking here and we'll invite you to one of our training session where you can get a feel for the club and sign up.
